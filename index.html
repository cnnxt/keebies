how to build your own ergo keyboard!

➡ tight shoulders and traps from hunching over your keyboard all day?

➡ tiny hands?

➡ need more macros and shortcuts?

➡ just want something fun to type on during your 9-5?

➡️ learn to build a little keeb for whatever you need!

it's easy!

step 1

figure out what form factor suits your needs (there are many variations to choose from!):

small hands?
look for low profile keyboards with "choc" spacing: the keys are spaced closer together, so your fingers won't have to move so far!
want it small and portable?
go for a keyboard with fewer keys: you can program the board to include whichever keys you need. this is a keyboard called the "sweep", a 34-key board.
want an integrated mouse?
look for one with an integrated trackball or touchpad!
